Environment v1.0
----------------

The application validates environment variables during startup.

Development defaults:
- NODE_ENV=development
- DB_SYNCHRONIZE=false
- DB_MIGRATIONS_RUN=true
- VPN_PROVIDER=noop
- VPN_3XUI_TLS_REJECT_UNAUTHORIZED=true
- PROVISION_CLEANUP_ENABLED=true
- PROVISION_CLEANUP_CRON="*/15 * * * *"

Production safety rules:
- DB_SYNCHRONIZE must be false.
- ADMIN_API_KEY must not be "secret".
- WEBHOOK_API_KEY must not be "secret".
- WEBHOOK_SIGNING_SECRET must not be "secret".
- DATA_ENCRYPTION_KEY must not be "base64-secret" or "secret".
- BILLING_API_KEY must not be "secret".
- STORAGE_ACCESS_KEY must not be "access".
- STORAGE_SECRET_KEY must not be "secret".
- VPN_3XUI_TLS_REJECT_UNAUTHORIZED=false is not allowed with a real VPN provider in production.

Validation checks:
- Required DB, Redis, admin, webhook, and encryption variables must be present.
- Boolean variables must be true or false.
- Numeric variables must be valid integers in allowed ranges.
- PROVISION_CLEANUP_CRON must be a 5-field cron expression.
- VPN_PROVIDER must be noop, 3x-ui, or threexui.

Local files:
- .env may override .env.example.
- .env must never be committed.
- .env.example is safe for development only.
